Introducing Elliot Atkinson

Creative Director at Ninety Percent

Atkinson is rethinking the needs of the Ninety Percent woman who is enjoying being out in the world again, at events, seeing friends, travelling, and relaxing. Dressing for different occasions and social gatherings, differentiating between day and evening again, are things we value more than ever. She appreciates craftsmanship and detail, from the fit of a dress to the placement of a pocket, a stitch, a bartack. Clothes cut from the finest materials, sculpted to the perfect fit, making her feel connected to the sensual world we live in.
 

The aesthetic language of Ninety Percent has evolved. Elliot has elevated it into a sophisticated vision for our customer. It’s about connecting the clothes with nature – capturing the sensuality of visiting uncharted destinations and feeling the sea, the earth. All whilst designing pieces that ultimately fit into the ebbs and flow of our current lifestyles.
 

This has all resulted in a thoughtfully designed collection of considered pieces in earth tones that can be paired together effortlessly and across the seasons. “Muted, tonal, earthy colours,” he says. “A blue would be like a dusty blue. It's very painterly.” His inspirations come from nature and the art world. His references range from the abstract expressionist Lee Krasner to the Modernist

sculptor Naum Gabo and the American Japanese artist Isamu Noguchi.Atkinson has honed his craft, having co-founded sustainable luxury brand  BITE Studios in Sweden. As creative director to the brand, he forged a unique aesthetic vision aligned with sustainable and ethical design practices, capturing a worldwide following of loyal customers. 
 

Now joining Ninety Percent as creative director, he seeks to create a consistent, modern, art and nature-inﬂuenced aesthetic language through his reﬁned handwriting.


Ninety Percent’s co-founders Shaﬁq Hassan and Para Hamilton created a visionary business model with deep-rooted values in people and the planet. 90% of our distributable proﬁts are shared with causes and the people that make our collections happen. 


Ninety Percent is more than a brand, it’s a community. From our headquarters to the factories, there is a connection between all the people behind our clothes. We know and respect our suppliers, factories and each garment maker in the value chain. This is how business should be done.
 

“I would like to invite you to be part of this positive new chapter on the brand’s journey towards a bright, sustainable future.”


Elliot Atkinson

IMPACT


We are committed to making sure that every place and person in our value chain is treated with dignity and respect, that workers are compensated fairly and that they are actively made aware of their value to us. We are PETA certified vegan and our clothes are ethically made mostly in the Echotex factory in Bangladesh, a leading facility both in terms of its environmental policy and its commitment to improving the wellbeing of its workforce. 

OUR WAY

 

We’re initiating industry change with our business culture – a pioneering proﬁt sharing model that reinvests 90% of its distributable proﬁts between the people who make our clothes and ﬁve charitable causes. We’re delivering luxury, responsibly-made fashion collections that empower people to rethink the value of proﬁts. The world we want is one of social development, rooted in caretaking for the planet and people.

SUSTAINABILITY


We prioritise the use of low-impact, renewable and certiﬁed organic materials when designing our collections. We are proud to choose GOTS-certiﬁed cotton yarns, renewable and biodegradable Tencel™, Cradle to Cradle™ fibres, natural linens and hemp, closed loop Tencel™ Lyocell and  Oeko-Tex certiﬁed SeaCell™. Our packaging is 100% plastic free, sent in eco-mailers made from 100% recycled cardboard and biodegradable cornstarch garment bags.
